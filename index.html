Must-Visit Places & Activities

Hagia Sophia

One of the most important monuments in the world, Hagia Sophia is a former Orthodox patriarchal basilica, later a mosque, and now a museum.

🕒 Suggested visit: 2-3 hours Book Tour

Blue Mosque

Also known as Sultan Ahmed Mosque, this architectural masterpiece features 20,000 handmade ceramic tiles and 200 stained glass windows.

🕒 Suggested visit: 1-2 hours 👔 Dress code applies

Grand Bazaar

One of the world's oldest and largest covered markets with over 4,000 shops offering traditional items, jewelry, and Turkish delights.

🕒 Suggested visit: 3-4 hours 💡 Remember to bargain!

Traditional Turkish Bath

Experience the centuries-old tradition of Turkish hammam at historical bathhouses.

🕒 Duration: 1.5 hours Book Experience

Food Walking Tour

Taste authentic Turkish cuisine from street food to traditional restaurants in local neighborhoods.

🕒 Duration: 4 hours 🍖 6 food stops included

Bosphorus Cruise

See Istanbul from the water while cruising between two continents, Europe and Asia.

🕒 Duration: 2 hours Book Cruise
